Us

The Meet
Adam and Rachel met the very first hour of the very first day of their pre-orientation program at Tufts University. We'd love to say it was love at first sight, but they were both in serious long term relationships with other people. Throughout college, they were best friends who studied together, vacationed together, and engaged in way too many Facebook pokes.
The Date
After graduation, they planned a road trip across the country together. After 3 weeks of 24/7 hiking, driving, camping, and site seeing, the two remained inseparable. Finally one of them (**cough** Rachel **cough**) got up the nerve to tell the other how she was feeling. Their first date was simultaneously incredibly awkward and incredibly comfortable, and thankfully, they found their groove.
The Proposal
Four years after that first date, the two were dining at their favorite restaurant, celebrating Adam's 27th birthday. After dessert, Adam got down on one knee in front of the entire restaurant and proposed. Rachel forgot to say yes, but he took the vigorous nodding as a good sign she was on board.
