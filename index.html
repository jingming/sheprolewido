Us

The Meet
Adam and Rachel met the very first hour of the very first day of their pre-orientation program at Tufts University. It would be wonderful to say it was love at first sight, but Rachel definitely thought that Adam didn't even like her. Thankfully, she was very wrong. Throughout college, they were best friends who studied together, vacationed together, and engaged in way too many Facebook pokes.
The Date
After graduation, they planned a road trip across the country together. After 3 weeks of 24/7 hiking, driving, camping, and site seeing, the two remained inseparable. Finally one of them (**cough** Rachel **cough**) got up the nerve to tell the other how she was feeling. Their first date was simultaneously incredibly awkward and incredibly comfortable, and thankfully, they found their groove.
The Proposal
Four years after that first date, the two were dining at their favorite restaurant, celebrating Adam's 27th birthday. After dessert, Adam got down on one knee in front of the entire restaurant and proposed. Rachel forgot to say yes, but he took the vigorous nodding as a good sign she was on board.

Your Plans

By Train
The hotel is a 30 second walk from the Charles MGH T stop on the red line. Charlie tickets can be purchased at any station.
By Car
Parking will be available at the hotel for a fee of $25 for the event and $52 over night. There are also a number of parking garages in the area a short walk from the venue.
Stay
We have reserved blocks of rooms or guaranteed rates at several local hotels.

The Liberty Hotel has reserved a block of rooms for us under the name "Lew Shepro Wedding" starting at $269 per night. Please call 866.961.3778 on or before March 28th to make a reservation in the block.

We have another room block at the Marriott in Kendall Square with rates starting at $249 per night. This hotel is a short public transit ride or a leisurely 20 minute walk across the bridge to the venue. Please call 617.494.6600 on or before April 6th to make a reservation under the "Lew / Shepro Wedding" room block.

Additionally, we have a guaranteed rate starting at $199 per night at the Porter Square Hotel, which is about a 15 minute public transit ride from the venue. Please call 617.499.3399 and book using the code "LEWPRO" to get our discounted rate.

If you would rather stay with more of the comforts of home, Boston also has an excellent selection of Airbnb's.
Sightsee
Walk the Freedom Trail, explore Faneuil Hall and Quincy Market Place, or take in the gorgeous ivy covered brick buildings of the South End. Boston is an incredibly walkable city with awesome restaurants and a rich history. We hope that you'll take the time to explore while you're here!
